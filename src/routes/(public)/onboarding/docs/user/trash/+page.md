# Trash

## Moving items to trash

From **Home**, you can move files or folders to **Trash** instead of deleting them immediately. Trashed items no longer appear in your normal Home listing.

## Trash view

Open **Trash** from the sidebar to see trashed items. Operations here are focused on recovery or permanent removal, depending on what the app exposes in the row actions.

## Background cleanup

The platform may run a scheduled job to purge expired trash according to server policy. Operators configure a secret and call the cron endpoint documented in the developer reference.
